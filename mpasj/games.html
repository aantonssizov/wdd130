Games

Quake Champions

Quake Champions currently is the most active and popular arena fps game. There are ongoing professional tournaments. Unlike other arena fps games QC features champions where each champion can significantly change the way you play the game. The cons of it are that it is purely optimized, there is no custom servers/maps/modes like in other arena fps.

Quake Live

Quake Live is second most popular quake. It has optimized engine and net-code. Features Quake 3 game play that is more tactical and you can calculate everything here and it always makes interesting duels there.

Quake 4

Quake 4 features quake 3-like game play with addition of crouch slides and also slow weapon respawn like in Quake 2. This combination makes the game very fast yet tactical. This also has the best version of ctf here thanks to crouch slide. Unfortunately it is the least active game in the genre and has not so good net-code as in other games.

QuakeWorld

Quake World is the first ever arena fps released back in 1996 and still active game thanks to being open source. It is also the fastest quake out there. Also has the best team mode arena fps 4on4 thanks to its deep tactics.
