Modes

Duel

Duel is the main competitive mode in any arena fps. Usually, it has a 10-minute time limit. The player who makes more frags as his opponent wins. The game has many concepts and the main ones are item timings, positioning, dealing damage, and dodging.

TDM 2vs2

2v2 is like duel but a with a teammate. It's more aim based than a duel. It's less stressful as you play not alone. And starting from this mode you also have quad damage - a power-up that can change how the game will go.

TDM 4v4

4v4 is also played like a duel but you play as a team of four which adds a new important skill being a team player.

CTF 4v4/5v5

CTF is like tdm but with objective - flags. The goal of each team is to capture the enemy flag and return back to your flag with it. Usually, players are separated into roles such as defense, middle, and attack.

FFA

FFA or DM is you versus everyone. The player who scored the most frags wins. It is the best mode to learn the basics of arena fps games. And of course, you can make a lot of cool frag streaks with quads :)
